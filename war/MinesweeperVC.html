Minesweeper

Instructions:
Click on the ? to learn how many mines are adjacent to that spot. If you guess wrong and blow up, the game is over!
If you give up, click Reveal to see the minefield. Click Restart to regenerate the minefield and get a new game.
Click View Scores to see your game totals (below the buttons); press View Scores again to refresh them.
Your score is the accumulated total of mines in games you have won.
